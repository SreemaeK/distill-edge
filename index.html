DISTILLEDGE

At DISTILLEDGE, we specialize in setting up complete IMFL bottling units and ethanol/spirit manufacturing plants from scratch to full-scale operation. With 30+ years of on-ground experience, we offer comprehensive, practical support at every stage of the project.

Our Services

Licensing & Regulatory Approvals
Licensing & Regulatory Approvals
  • Obtaining all the required licenses and permissions for setting up IMFL/ethanol/spirit units.
Site Selection & Factory Planning
Site Selection & Factory Planning
  • Help in identifying and evaluating suitable land based on utility access, local laws, and logistics.
  • Complete factory design and layout planning.
  • Support for optimized construction planning with focus on cost-efficiency and quality.
Machinery & Equipment Setup
Machinery & Equipment Setup
  • Recommendation and selection of machinery based on plant capacity and client needs.
  • Preparation of detailed equipment specs (e.g. nozzle diameter, filling speed, etc.)
  • Electrical, piping, and utility layout planning.
  • Sourcing competitive quotations from verified equipment suppliers.
  • Assistance with PO (purchase order) placement and vendor negotiations.
Project Execution & Commissioning
Project Execution & Commissioning
  • Vendor coordination and follow-up to ensure timely delivery of equipment.
  • On-site supervision for equipment installation and commissioning.
  • Execution of trial runs to validate system performance.
Operational Setup
Operational Setup
  • Manpower planning and recruitment support for plant operations.
  • Arranging tie-ups with brand owners for bottling (tie-up/sub-lease agreements).
  • Support in preparing documentation for sub-leasing or brand collaboration.
Financial Planning & Loan Assistance
Financial Planning & Loan Assistance
  • Prepares detailed project reports (DPR) for bank loans.
  • Approaching banks and financial institutions for funding.
  • Financial modelling and projections for 10 years (eg : volume sold, costs incurred, profits, breakeven analysis).
